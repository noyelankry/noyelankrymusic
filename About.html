About

Noy Elankry is a 25 years-old Israeli musician. Noy started her musical journey as a guitarist, but after 7 years of pure guitar playing, she got into singing and started posting covers on her Instagram account. Last year she started to write songs and learn how to produce them, which lead to her participation in Yahel Doron's creation course. She has recently released two singles and is currently working on her debut album.

Noy's first single was produced and arranged by musician Yahel Doron from the duo "Guy and Yahel". Noy is the writer and composer of her own music, and her second single was produced by her. She aspires to produce more music in the near future. Please see the links below for more information!
